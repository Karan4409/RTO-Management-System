Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

To apply for a learner's license in India, you need to be at least 16 years old for a motorcycle with an engine capacity not exceeding 50cc, and at least 18 years old for other vehicles.

You will need to provide proof of age and residence, along with a passport-sized photograph and a medical certificate (for commercial license applicants). The specific documents required may vary depending on your state of residence.

The processing time for a driver's license in India varies depending on the state and the type of license. Generally, it takes around 1-2 months to get a permanent license after passing the necessary tests.

A driver's license in India is valid for 20 years from the date of issue or until the age of 50, whichever comes earlier. After that, it needs to be renewed every 5 years.

If you fail the written or driving test in India, you will be allowed to retake the test after a certain period of time has passed. The specific rules and requirements may vary depending on your state of residence.
